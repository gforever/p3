KAIJU CREATOR

Welcome to the Kaiju Creator. Here you can build your own Kaiju with various Kaiju parts. Create your ultimate monster! Best Viewed in Full Screen.

Instructions:

Select a background. You can choose various colors, textures, or a background image. Selecting a texture image allows you to change the background color as well whereas a background image will not be affected by the background color.
Click to clone the kaiju part onto the canvas. Drag the cloned , clear canvas with start over button, change background, create a name for your kaiju with length limitation, and save image.

Extra Features:

Drop cloned item to recycle bin, tooltip, resizing cloned parts, custom cursor icons. Save image without trash can icon and maintain droppable functionality.

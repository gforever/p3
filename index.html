KAIJU CREATOR

Welcome to the Kaiju Creator. Here you can build your own Kaiju with various Kaiju parts. Create your ultimate kaiju! Best Viewed in Full Screen.

Instructions:

  • Select a background. You can choose various colors, textures, or a background image. You can combine the texture and background color to create a unique combination. A background image will not be affected by the background color.
  • Click to clone the kaiju part onto the canvas. In the canvas, you can drag the cloned part to build your kaiju.
  • When you are finished, you can output your kaiju to a new image in a new window that can be saved.
  • You also have the option of starting over by clearing the canvas.

Extra Features:

  • You can drag a cloned part to the trash bin to drop it off your canvas.
  • Hovering over certain areas of the app will provide you with some instructions with the tooltip.
  • You also have the ability to resize cloned parts on the canvas.
  • Cursor icons change when hovering over certain areas of the app.
  • When you save an image, it will hide the trash icon and maintain the droppable functionality if you decide to continue using the app.
